Upload Instructions

(*NOTE: All images uploaded are displayed with dimensions of 331x496 pixels, or a multiple of this.)

  1. Navigate to the Theta Tau Miami shared Google Drive
  2. Follow the folder path of Pictures > Website > Headshots
  3. Upload your professional headshot to the Google Drive folder, preferrably cropped to dimensions of 331x496 pixels
  4. Double-click on your uploaded photo, click on the three dots in the top-right corner, and select "Share"
  5. Look at the bottom of the Share menu and change the General Access section to "Anyone with the link" with "Viewer" privilages
  6. After selecting the correct options, copy the link and paste it into the submission box down below
