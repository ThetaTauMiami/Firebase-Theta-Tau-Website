Upload Instructions

*NOTE: All images uploaded are displayed with dimensions of 331x496 pixels, or a multiple of this.

  1. Navigate to the Theta Tau Miami shared Google Drive
  2. Follow the folder path: Pictures > Website > Headshots
  3. Upload your professional headshot (preferably cropped to 331x496 pixels)
  4. Double-click on your uploaded photo, click on the three dots in the top-right corner, and select "Share"
  5. Change the General Access setting to "Anyone with the link" and set permissions to "Viewer"
  6. Copy the link and paste it into the submission box below
