Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I rush Theta Tau?

Joining Theta Tau enables you to connect with other students who are also passionate about engineering. We value close bonds and friendships by hosting brotherhood events such as card game nights, big-little family dinners, and our annual formal. Theta Tau also offers opportunities to give back through community service and to grow professionally through networking events and resume/interview workshops.

Who can join Theta Tau?

Students pursuing a degree in the College of Engineering and Computing (CEC) at Miami University are eligible to join:

  • Biomedical Engineering
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Computer Engineering
  • Computer Science
  • Cybersecurity
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Robotics Engineering
  • Software Engineering
  • Smart Manufacturing Engineering
When is Theta Tau recruitment?

Theta Tau recruitment begins at the end of September.

How long is the recruitment process?

The recruitment process takes two weeks. During that time, you will be attending social and professional events to get to know our members.

I am interested in rushing Theta Tau. What should I do?

We host three information session in early September. You must attend at least one info session to be eligible to rush. You can find us at Mega Fair to learn more about us, too.
